Clubs and bars

Embrace the pulsating rhythm of Trondheim's nightlife, where the city's vibrant energy comes to life after dark. Specifically tailored for students, the club and bar scene in Trondheim is a thrilling blend of music, camaraderie, and unforgettable experiences. Whether you're seeking the beat of the dance floor or a cozy corner for spirited conversations, Trondheim's clubs and bars offer an array of options to suit every taste and mood.

Studentersamfundet

The Studentersamfundet (samf) has countless of bars and clubs within it’s red walls. Here you can dance the night away in the Bodega or Klubben or have a few beers at Strossa or Edgar. As a volunteer at samf, you also have access to the secret club rooms called "hyblene". Samf has a variety of bars and clubs with an age limit of 18 and 20.

Downtown

Downtown is located in the city center and has a minimum age limit of 20 years. The club is well know for hosting the best parties on Thursdays which is better know as “DT-torsdag”.

Lokal bar

If you are really into the tecno scene and also enjoy trying experimental and fancy drinks, Lokal bar is the place for you. It is located in the city center and has a minimum age limit of 20 years.

Barmuda

Barmuda is famous for their “cava sundays” where you can buy a bottle of cava for much cheaper than usual. The bar is located at Solsiden and has an age limit of 20 years. It’s a perfect place to meet familiar faces over a glass (or bottle) of cava.

Tag

Tag is located in on of the main shopping streets in the city center. Because of it’s dance floor in the basement, it’s the perfect place for people how like to let loose and dance. During the hours of the day it is a restaurant so that you can grab a bite to eat before the party starts!
